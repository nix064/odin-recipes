Spicy Shoyu Ramen

Ingredients

Broth Ingredients

  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Shiitake mushrooms
  • Spicy chili bean paste (optional; use this if you want a kick!)
  • Chicken or vegetable stock
  • Dashi stock
  • Low sodium soy sauce
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Sea salt
  • Sugar

Toppings

  • Soft-boiled egg
  • Carrots
  • Scallions
  • Narutomaki
  • White pepper
  • Protein of your choice
  • Sesame seeds

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add the garlic and ginger.
  3. Stir the garlic and ginger around and let them simmer for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms to the saucepan.
  5. Saut√© the mushrooms until they have softened.
  6. Drop the chili paste into the pot and stir it around to coat the mushrooms.
  7. Stir in the soy sauce, rice wine vinegar, salt, and sugar.
  8. Pour both the chicken and dashi stock into the pot.
  9. Give everything a good stir.
  10. Let the soup simmer for about 15 minutes.
  11. While the soup is simmering, cook your ramen noodles separately according to their package instructions.
  12. Prepare your desired toppings for the ramen.
  13. Once the soup has simmered for 15 minutes, give it a taste test and adjust the heat and soy sauce according to your preference.
  14. When the noodles are cooked, strain them and transfer them to a large bowl, preferably a ramen bowl.
  15. Pour some of the broth from the saucepan into the bowl over the noodles.
  16. Garnish the bowl with your chosen toppings.
  17. Your ramen is now ready to be enjoyed!
